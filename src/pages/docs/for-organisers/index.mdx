## Why List With Us?

When you list with Giveback Guide, you gain:

- **Quality over quantity** — Volunteers who research carefully and commit seriously
- **No listing fees** — We never charge legitimate non-profits or community projects
- **Respectful partnership** — We amplify your voice, not speak over it
- **Detailed vetting** — Our thorough process builds traveller trust in your legitimacy
- **Long-term relationship** — We support your work, not just list it

## Who Can List

### Volunteer Projects

We list organisations running genuine volunteer opportunities including:

- Conservation and environmental projects
- Community development initiatives
- Education and skills training programs
- Animal welfare and rehabilitation centres
- Cultural heritage preservation
- Social justice and empowerment work
- Health and medical outreach

### Sustainable Stays

We feature accommodation that genuinely demonstrates:

- Community benefit and local ownership
- Cultural preservation
- Environmental responsibility
- Transparent, honest sustainability claims
- Fair economic practices

### What We Don't List

We explicitly exclude:

- Orphanage volunteering programs
- Wildlife tourism that exploits animals (rides, shows, photo ops)
- Projects with no local leadership or community involvement
- Organisations that cannot demonstrate genuine impact
- Greenwashing operations without substance
- For-profit voluntourism companies

## Our Standards

Every organisation we list must meet our rigorous standards. Before applying, please 
review:

- [Our verification process](/docs/policies/verification/) — What we assess and how
- [Animal Welfare Policy](/docs/policies/animal-welfare/) — Required for any project involving animals
- [Our mission and charter](/docs/about/mission-charter/) — The values we uphold

## The Application Process

Listing with Giveback Guide involves several stages:

1. **Initial application** — Submit detailed information about your organisation
2. **Documentation review** — We examine your legal status, financials, and impact evidence
3. **Remote assessment** — Video calls with your team, reference checks
4. **Site visit** — Where possible, we visit to verify operations
5. **Listing creation** — If approved, we create your listing with your input
6. **Ongoing monitoring** — Continuous evaluation to ensure standards are maintained

[Read the complete guide to getting listed](/docs/for-organisers/how-to-be-listed/).
