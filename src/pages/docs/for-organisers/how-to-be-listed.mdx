<p class="lead">
Thank you for your interest in listing with Giveback Guide. This guide walks you through 
our application process, what we look for, and how to prepare a strong application.
</p>

## Before You Apply

Please ensure your organisation meets our basic criteria:

- ✓ Legally registered non-profit, NGO, or community organisation (or legitimate sustainable business for stays)
- ✓ Genuine, measurable impact on communities, animals, or environment
- ✓ Local leadership and community involvement
- ✓ Transparent operations and finances
- ✓ No involvement in prohibited activities (orphanage tourism, animal exploitation, etc.)
- ✓ Commitment to ethical practices and continuous improvement

[Review our full verification criteria](/docs/policies/verification/) before proceeding.

## Step 1: Initial Application

Complete our online application form. You'll need to provide:

### Basic Information

- Organisation name and legal status
- Year founded and registration details
- Primary contact person and role
- Website and social media links

### Mission and Impact

- Mission statement and primary goals
- Description of your work and approach
- Communities, animals, or environments you serve
- Evidence of impact (data, reports, testimonials)
- How you measure success

### Volunteer/Stays Information

- Description of volunteer roles or accommodation offered
- Skills and experience required
- Time commitments and availability
- Costs involved (program fees, accommodation, etc.)
- What's included (meals, accommodation, training, support)

### Operations and Governance

- Organisational structure and leadership
- Staff qualifications and training
- Annual budget and funding sources
- Financial transparency practices
- Safeguarding and child protection policies (if applicable)

## Step 2: Documentation Submission

After initial application, you'll be asked to submit supporting documents:

### Required Documents

- **Legal registration** — Certificate of incorporation, charity registration, etc.
- **Financial statements** — Recent annual reports or audited accounts
- **Insurance certificates** — Liability, property, and volunteer insurance
- **Permits and licenses** — Required for your operations
- **Safeguarding policy** — If working with vulnerable populations
- **References** — From partner organisations, previous volunteers, or community members

### Impact Evidence

- Annual impact reports
- Case studies or success stories
- Third-party evaluations or audits
- Media coverage or press mentions
- Academic research or studies

## Step 3: Assessment and Interviews

### Remote Assessment

Our team will conduct:

- **Video interviews** — With key staff and leadership
- **Facility tour** — Live video walkthrough of your site
- **Reference checks** — We'll contact your references directly
- **Community verification** — Where possible, we'll speak with local partners

### Site Visit

Where logistically possible, a Giveback Guide team member or trusted local partner will 
visit your organisation unannounced to:

- Observe operations as they normally run
- Verify facilities match documentation
- Speak informally with staff and volunteers
- Assess living conditions for volunteers (if applicable)
- Evaluate genuine impact and community relationships

## Step 4: Decision

We will notify you of our decision within [timeframe] of completing assessment. 
Possible outcomes:

### Approved

Congratulations! We'll create your listing with your input on:

- Project/stay description and highlights
- Photo selection and optimisation
- Requirements and logistics information
- Impact story and verification details

### Revision Requested

Your application shows promise but needs work in specific areas. We'll provide detailed 
feedback on what's needed and give you [timeframe] to address concerns.

### Declined

Unfortunately, your organisation doesn't currently meet our standards. We'll explain why 
and whether changes could lead to future approval.

## Tips for a Strong Application

### Be Transparent

Honesty matters more than perfection. We appreciate organisations that:

- Acknowledge challenges and limitations
- Share failures alongside successes
- Demonstrate learning and adaptation
- Provide evidence, not just claims

### Demonstrate Local Leadership

We prioritise organisations that:

- Are led by local community members
- Have community advisory boards
- Can show community support and involvement
- Empower rather than create dependency

### Show Clear Impact

Strong applications demonstrate:

- Specific, measurable outcomes
- How you track and evaluate success
- Sustainability of impact beyond volunteer involvement
- Long-term vision and planning

## After Listing

### Ongoing Requirements

Once listed, you must:

- Keep your listing information current and accurate
- Respond to volunteer inquiries promptly
- Participate in our feedback and review system
- Notify us of significant changes (leadership, operations, closure)
- Undergo re-verification every [time period]

### Support We Provide

- Regular check-ins and feedback
- Resources on best practices
- Access to our network of ethical organisations
- Promotion through our channels
- Assistance with any issues or concerns
