## Before You Begin

Before diving into searches, take some time to reflect on what you're looking for. 
Consider:

- **Your skills and experience** — What can you genuinely contribute?
- **Your time availability** — Some projects require weeks or months
- **Your budget** — Many projects have costs; factor these in
- **Your comfort zone** — Rural vs. urban, basic vs. comfortable
- **Your goals** — Learning, contributing, both?

## Creating an Account

While you can browse Giveback Guide without an account, creating one gives you access to:

- Save favourite projects and stays
- Create custom lists for different trips
- Leave reviews and share experiences
- Receive personalised recommendations
- Track your volunteer history

[Sign up here](/login) — it's free and takes less than a minute.

## Our Commitment to You

When you use Giveback Guide, you can trust that:

- Every listing has been thoroughly researched and verified
- We never accept payment for listings or preferential placement
- Our verification process is transparent and rigorous
- We remove organisations that no longer meet our standards
- Your reviews and feedback directly improve the platform
