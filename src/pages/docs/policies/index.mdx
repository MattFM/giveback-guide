## Core Principles

All our policies are built on these foundational principles:

- **Do No Harm** — First, ensure the project doesn't cause damage
- **Local Ownership** — Communities must lead their own development
- **Transparency** — Information must be honest and complete
- **Sustainability** — Impact must outlast volunteer visits
- **Dignity** — All people and animals treated with respect

## Policy Updates

Our policies evolve as we learn and as best practices develop. When we make significant 
changes, we notify organisations on our platform and update the version dates on policy pages. 
We welcome feedback from communities, experts, and travellers on how we can improve.
