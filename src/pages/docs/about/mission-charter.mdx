<p class="lead">
Giveback Guide exists to transform how people travel and volunteer, creating a world where 
exploration and positive impact go hand in hand. This charter defines who we are, what we 
stand for, and the principles that guide every decision we make.
</p>

## Our Mission

> To connect travellers and volunteers with genuine, ethical opportunities that create 
> meaningful positive impact—while ensuring dignity, sustainability, and benefit flow 
> primarily to local communities, animals, and environments.

We believe travel should be a force for good. Every journey presents an opportunity to 
learn, contribute, and leave places better than we found them. Our platform bridges the 
gap between well-meaning travellers and legitimate, impactful organisations that are 
doing remarkable work around the world.

## What We Stand For

### 1. Authentic Impact Over Performative Activism

We reject voluntourism that prioritises the volunteer's experience over genuine community 
benefit. Every project we list must demonstrate measurable, sustainable outcomes that 
primarily serve local needs—not the Instagram feeds of visitors.

### 2. Local Leadership and Ownership

The best projects are those designed, led, and sustained by local communities. We 
prioritise organisations that empower local people as decision-makers, not just beneficiaries. 
When external support is needed, it should enhance local capacity, not replace it.

### 3. Transparency in Everything

We commit to radical transparency about how we operate, how we verify projects, and what 
travellers can realistically expect. No greenwashing. No hidden fees. No overstated impact 
claims.

### 4. Animal Welfare as Non-Negotiable

We hold ourselves to the highest standards regarding animal welfare. Any project involving 
animals must prioritise animal wellbeing above all else—including visitor satisfaction and 
financial sustainability. [Read our full Animal Welfare Policy](/docs/policies/animal-welfare/).

### 5. Environmental Responsibility

The climate crisis demands that travel itself be reimagined. We prioritise sustainable 
stays, advocate for slow travel, and seek projects that actively contribute to environmental 
restoration and conservation.

## Our Commitments

### To Travellers

- **Honest representation** — What you see is what you'll experience
- **Verified opportunities** — Every listing meets our rigorous standards
- **Clear expectations** — No surprises about costs, requirements, or outcomes
- **Support and guidance** — Resources to help you prepare and reflect

### To Project Organisers

- **Respectful partnership** — We amplify your voice, not speak over it
- **Fair representation** — Your work described accurately and with dignity
- **Supportive matching** — Connecting you with volunteers who fit your needs
- **Zero listing fees** — We never charge organisations to be featured

### To Communities and Environments

- **Do no harm** — Rigorous vetting prevents exploitative practices
- **Centre local voices** — Communities define what help they need
- **Protect vulnerable populations** — Especially strict standards for projects involving children or animals
- **Long-term thinking** — Prioritising sustainability over short-term gains

## What We Reject

In service of our mission, we explicitly do not support:

- **Orphanage tourism** — We never list projects involving visits to orphanages or institutional care facilities
- **Wildlife exploitation** — No elephant rides, tiger selfies, or photo opportunities that stress or endanger animals
- **Short-term voluntourism** — Projects requiring minimal training or time that primarily benefit the volunteer's ego
- **Greenwashing** — Sustainability claims without substance or evidence
- **Extractive tourism** — Experiences that take more than they give to local communities

## How We're Funded

Giveback Guide operates sustainably without compromising our values. Our funding comes from:

- Affiliate partnerships with booking platforms (clearly disclosed)
- Optional traveller contributions and memberships
- Grants from foundations aligned with our mission
- Consulting services for organisations seeking ethical tourism guidance

**We never:** Charge organisations to be listed, sell user data, accept payment for 
preferential placement, or compromise our verification standards for revenue.
