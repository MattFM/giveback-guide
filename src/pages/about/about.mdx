Give Back Guide is growing database of travel experiences, tours and accommodation, but with a twist.

All of the listings on our platform have a positive impact on people, places and the planet. Many are social enterprises or community projects, some are co-ops, others are non-profits or just regular businesses with a commitment to do something good for their local communities or ecosystems.

As well as regular tourist-orientated things (like tours, hotels, retreats, museums and experiences), we also have cafes, shops and restaurants. Everything you would normally do on a trip, but with added positive impact for the places you love to visit.

Give Back Guide is a non-profit organisation. We are not for sale. We exist for the public good.

## Why we exist

The travel industry is currently run, in general, by placing most of the emphasis on what the traveller wants. This leads to a multitude of issues.

Over-tourism, financial leakage, community and cultural erosion, disproportionately high carbon emissions, animal exploitation, the list goes on and on…

Give Back Guide operates on the belief and assumption that regenerative travel is the answer (or at least part of the answer) to so many of these issues. 

To travel regeneratively is to leave a place in a better state than you found it. Regenerative travel shifts the focus from the individual traveller to the destination.

## How Give Back Guide came to be

Give Back Guide was founded by Matt & Jade (that’s us). Whilst on a six month tour of Southeast Asia, we found ourselves wanting our travels to be more meaningful. We found ourselves gravitating towards social enterprise cafes and shops, community litters picks and animal sanctuaries. We immediately felt the difference. We were meeting awesome local people who were passionate and actively involved in their communities and our travels were having a positive impact on the places we visited. It was a win-win!

We were however surprised by how much effort and time it took to find projects to engage with, so we started to collate all the projects we found. We soon realised that our collection might be useful to others, so we released it publicly for free.

From that moment, Give Back Guide was born.

## Our Values

### Transparency & Integrity

We believe in open communication and we want our users to feel like they can trust us. We can help to bring this about by being open with how we are funded and how we run our business.

### Regeneration & Sustainability

We want tourism to be a tool for regeneration and we exist to provide a means for people to bring this about. In short, we want the world to be left in a better state than we found it.

We aim for our business practices to be genuinely sustainable, the details of which can be found in our Climate Action Plan.

### Equity

We want society to progress, and we don’t want anyone to be left out. We will challenge systems that create imbalance, lift up historically marginalised communities and always ask ourselves whether we can do better.

### Community

Our work is rooted in the belief that meaningful change happens when people come together. We intend to listen, to learn and to collaborate. We recognise that we are a part of a wider network, and that collective action has greater effect.
